## Agency-Standard Keywords

### 1. Lighting (The "Mood" Layer)
- `Volumetric lighting` — rays of light through air/smoke.
- `Rim lighting` — sharp outline around product; essential for tech/cars.
- `Cinematic Caustics` — realistic light refractions through glass/water.
- `High-Contrast Chiaroscuro` — dramatic, moody luxury for jewelry/liquor.
- `Museum-grade spot lighting` — focused, clean light for high-end retail.

### 2. Physical Textures (The "Realism" Layer)
- **Metals:** `Brushed titanium`, `Cold-rolled steel`, `Polished chrome`, `Anodized aluminum`.
- **Natural:** `Porous limestone`, `Raw obsidian`, `Dark walnut wood grain`, `White marble`.
- **Commercial:** `Matte rubber`, `Frozen condensation droplets`, `Gold-leaf foil stamping`, `High-gloss glass`.

### 3. Camera & Lens (The "Elite" Layer)
- `100mm Macro` — extreme detail (skin pores, fabric weave, liquid drops).
- `Phase One XF / Hasselblad H6D` — forces ultra-high resolution medium format textures.
- `35mm Film Grain` — for authentic lifestyle/fashion (Gucci/Vogue style).
- `Phantom Flex4K` — for ultra-high-speed liquid/splash physics.
