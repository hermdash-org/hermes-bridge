# Professional Agency Production Guide

Use these formulas to generate commercial-grade assets that replace traditional photoshoots and CGI teams.

## The Expanded Golden Formula

*For any product in any of the 1 million+ business fields.*

```
[Shot Type] of [Product/Brand] in [Environment], resting on [Surface], featuring [Macro Textures].
Lit by [Lighting Style], shot on [Professional Camera/Lens]. [Negative Constraints].
```

## Agency-Standard Keywords

### 1. Lighting (The "Mood" Layer)
- `Volumetric lighting` — rays of light through air/smoke.
- `Rim lighting` — sharp outline around product; essential for tech/cars.
- `Cinematic Caustics` — realistic light refractions through glass/water.
- `High-Contrast Chiaroscuro` — dramatic, moody luxury for jewelry/liquor.
- `Museum-grade spot lighting` — focused, clean light for high-end retail.

### 2. Physical Textures (The "Realism" Layer)
- **Metals:** `Brushed titanium`, `Cold-rolled steel`, `Polished chrome`, `Anodized aluminum`.
- **Natural:** `Porous limestone`, `Raw obsidian`, `Dark walnut wood grain`, `White marble`.
- **Commercial:** `Matte rubber`, `Frozen condensation droplets`, `Gold-leaf foil stamping`, `High-gloss glass`.

### 3. Camera & Lens (The "Elite" Layer)
- `100mm Macro` — extreme detail (skin pores, fabric weave, liquid drops).
- `Phase One XF / Hasselblad H6D` — forces ultra-high resolution medium format textures.
- `35mm Film Grain` — for authentic lifestyle/fashion (Gucci/Vogue style).
- `Phantom Flex4K` — for ultra-high-speed liquid/splash physics.

## Specialized Marketing Templates

**The "Mindblowing" Impossible Shot (Liquid/CGI):**
> "High-speed macro photography of [Product] colliding with a splash of [Liquid Type]. Symmetrical crown-shaped splash, razor-sharp focus on ripples. Cinematic studio lighting. Shot on Phantom Flex4K. Pure luxury CGI aesthetic."

**Physical Typography (Announcements):**
> "Professional photography of the word '[TEXT]' rendered as massive 3D [Material: Matte Black/Glass/Chrome] letters. Resting in a minimalist [Environment]. Dramatic side-lighting creating long realistic shadows. 8k resolution, crisp typography, no glow."

**OOH Billboard (The "Wild" Shot):**
> "Wide-angle professional photography of a massive digital billboard in [Location: Times Square/London]. Billboard displays a minimalist ad for [Product]. Realistic city reflections on wet asphalt. 8k, photorealistic, professional OOH campaign."

**The "Crunch/Sizzle" (Food/CPG):**
> "Commercial food photography of [Food Item]. Extreme macro focus on [Texture: Crispy skin/Fizzing bubbles]. Shot on 100mm macro. Dramatic backlighting. Indistinguishable from a global TV commercial."

## The "Anti-AI" Negative Prompt

*Always append this to ensure professional output.*

```
(gibberish text, malformed letters, neon glow, sci-fi, cyberpunk, hologram, 3D render, cartoon,
plastic texture, over-saturated, glowing edges, watermark, fake-looking, blurry text, messy lines)
```
