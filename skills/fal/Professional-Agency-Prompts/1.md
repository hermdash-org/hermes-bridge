# 📑 Master Reference: The "Agency-Standard" AI Prompting Guide

## 1. The "Golden Formula" for 1 Million Fields
To generate a professional ad for *any* product in *any* industry, use this exact structure:

> **[Shot Type]** of a **[Main Subject/Product]** with **[Brand Name]**, resting on **[Surface/Environment]**, featuring **[Specific Material Textures]**. Lit by **[Lighting Style]**, shot on **[Professional Camera/Lens]**. **[Negative Constraints]**.

---

## 2. The Professional Keyword Library
Mix and match these technical terms to ensure the result looks like a $10,000 photoshoot, not a "generated" image.

### A. Lighting (The most important part)
*   **Volumetric Lighting:** To show rays of light through air.
*   **Rim Lighting:** To create a thin, sharp outline of light around the product (makes it "pop").
*   **Softbox/Diffused Lighting:** For soft, elegant shadows (best for skincare/beauty).
*   **High-Contrast/Chiaroscuro:** For dramatic, moody, luxury vibes (best for watches/liquor).
*   **Golden Hour:** Natural, warm, orange-tinted sunlight.

### B. Camera & Lenses (The "Pro" Look)
*   **85mm or 100mm Macro:** For extreme close-ups with sharp detail.
*   **35mm Film:** For a "raw," authentic, lifestyle look with a slight grain.
*   **Hasselblad / Phase One XF:** These are $50k cameras. Naming them forces the AI to use ultra-high resolution textures.
*   **Shallow Depth of Field (f/1.4 or f/1.8):** This blurs the background beautifully so the product stands out.

### C. Texture & Realism (The "No-Glow" Secret)
*   **Material focus:** "Brushed titanium," "Matte-finish," "Porous limestone," "Microscopic skin pores," "Cold condensation," "Visible fabric weave."
*   **Imperfections:** "Subtle dust particles in light," "natural reflections," "realistic shadows," "un-retouched."

## 3. Industry-Specific Templates (Reference Examples)

### Field: High-End Technology (Hardware/SaaS)
> **Prompt:** "Minimalist commercial photography of a **[Product Name]** on a brushed aluminum surface. Harsh top-down studio lighting. Sharp focus on the metallic edges. Shot on Sony A7R IV, 100mm macro lens. No glowing lines, no sci-fi effects, clean corporate aesthetic."

### Field: Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (Food/Beverage)
> **Prompt:** "High-speed splash photography of **[Product Name]** with hyper-realistic water droplets and ice shards. 8k resolution, cinematic lighting, vibrant colors. Shot on 1/8000 shutter speed. Razor-sharp focus on the liquid texture."

### Field: Luxury Goods (Jewelry/Fashion)
> **Prompt:** "Editorial beauty shot of **[Product Name]** on a dark velvet background. Dramatic side-lighting. Deep shadows, elegant highlights on the gold and gemstone surfaces. Shot on Hasselblad H6D. Hyper-realistic reflections."

---

## 4. The "Business Automation" Negative Prompt
Whenever you want to ensure a professional look without the "AI weirdness," always add this to the end of your prompt or in your settings:

> **Negative Prompt:** "gibberish text, malformed letters, neon glow, sci-fi, cyberpunk, hologram, 3D render, cartoon, digital art, plastic texture, over-saturated, glowing edges, blurry foreground, low resolution, watermark."

---

## 5. How to Expand to "1 Million Fields"
If a business owner asks you for something obscure (e.g., "I sell high-end industrial drill bits"), simply plug it into the **Golden Formula**:

1.  **Object:** Industrial Drill Bit.
2.  **Surface:** Rusty iron plate or Blueprint paper.
3.  **Lighting:** Harsh workshop fluorescent light.
4.  **Lens:** Macro lens (to show the sharp steel edges).
5.  **Result:** A professional, rugged, B2B industrial ad that looks like a high-end catalog.
